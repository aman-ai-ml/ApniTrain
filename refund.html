Refund Policy

Effective Date: October 2024

1. Introduction

At Apni Train, we aim to provide a seamless and hassle-free booking experience. However, we understand that plans can change. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions for ticket cancellations and refunds.

2. Cancellation Eligibility

Refunds are available for tickets that meet the following conditions:

  • Tickets must be canceled at least 24 hours before the scheduled departure time.
  • Refunds are only applicable for bookings made directly through the Apni Train website or mobile app.
  • Certain promotional or discounted tickets may be non-refundable. Please check the terms at the time of booking.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your Apni Train account and navigate to the "My Bookings" section.
  • Select the ticket you wish to cancel and click on "Cancel Booking."
  • Once the cancellation is confirmed, the refund process will begin.
  • You will receive an email confirmation regarding the cancellation and refund status.

4. Refund Timeline

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days from the date of cancellation. The refunded amount will be credited to the original payment method used at the time of booking. Please note that the time taken for the refund to reflect in your account may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

5. Refund Amount

The refund amount will be calculated based on the following:

  • A cancellation fee may be applied depending on the fare type and time of cancellation.
  • Taxes and service fees are generally non-refundable.
  • The remaining balance, after deducting the applicable fees, will be refunded to you.

6. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • If the ticket is canceled less than 24 hours before departure.
  • For "No Show" passengers who do not board the train without canceling the ticket.
  • For tickets purchased under non-refundable promotions or special offers.

7. Modification of Tickets

If you wish to modify your booking (e.g., change the travel date or time), cancellation and rebooking will be required. The cancellation will be subject to the refund policy, and the new booking will be made at the current rates.
