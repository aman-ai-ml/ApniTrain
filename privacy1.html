Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Apni Train's Privacy Policy page. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and use our services. By accessing Apni Train, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information from users: personal and non-personal data.

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information to provide services and improve user experience. This includes:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you register or book tickets.
  • Payment information, including credit or debit card details, to complete transactions.
  • Location data when you use location-based services such as finding nearby train stations.
  • Travel preferences and feedback to enhance your journey experience.

2.2 Non-Personal Information

Non-personal data is collected automatically as you interact with our website. This includes:

  • IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • Browsing patterns, pages visited, and time spent on the website.
  • Cookies to track your session and improve navigation. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

3. Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To process bookings, handle payments, and provide you with train schedules and notifications.
  • To create and manage your account, and to offer customer support.
  • To personalize and enhance your experience on Apni Train, based on your preferences.
  • To analyze website usage and improve its functionality and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and to detect or prevent fraudulent activities.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data such as payment information.
  • Strict access controls, ensuring only authorized personnel can access personal data.
  • Regular security audits and software updates to prevent vulnerabilities.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted partners under the following conditions:

  • With third-party service providers who assist us in operating the website, processing payments, or providing customer support.
  • To comply with legal requirements, such as responding to subpoenas or court orders.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, and that of our users or the public.

6. Your Rights and Choices

As a user, you have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request corrections or updates to your data.
  • The right to request the deletion of your data, subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  • The ability to opt out of marketing communications or revoke consent at any time.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow us to recognize your preferences. You can choose to block cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may limit your ability to use certain features of the website.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Afterward, we securely delete or anonymize the data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website if the changes are significant. It is your responsibility to review the privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
