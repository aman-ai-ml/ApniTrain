    Frequently Asked Questions


    What should I do after booking a ticket?

    After booking a ticket, you can see your ticket in the My Booking section in the app or website. You will get the verification message on your mobile via SMS and WhatsApp.

    Which train information is available on the ApniTrain Website and App?

    Apnitrain provides information on RSRTC. Government, Lok Parivahan, and private buses. It includes Train number, AC status, Seat availability (including sleeper and sharing sleeper options), Train departure time and timetable, and complete Train routes

    How will the Train conductor verify the ticket?

    Tickets booked through the ApniTrain app have a unique PNR number and QR code. The TT will check and verify the ticket by scanning the QR code or PNR

    Can I get a refund if my traveling plans are canceled?

    If you cancel a ticket, you will receive a refund according to the cancellation policy
